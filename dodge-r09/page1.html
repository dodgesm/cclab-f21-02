The Laughing Heart by Charels Bukowski

Description

"The Laughing Heart" urges the reader to take control of their own life and sieze opportunities making the most of their time. The general idea of the poem is life is boring or pointless, unless someone actively tries to change it.

A Reading of The Laughing Heart by Tom O'Bedlam

The Laughing Heart


your life is your life
don’t let it be clubbed into dank submission.
be on the watch.
there are ways out.
there is light somewhere.
it may not be much light but
it beats the darkness.
be on the watch.
the gods will offer you chances.
know them.
take them.
you can’t beat death but
you can beat death in life, sometimes.
and the more often you learn to do it,
the more light there will be.
your life is your life.
know it while you have it.
you are marvelous
the gods wait to delight
in you.
--by Charles Bukowski
